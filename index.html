Apple Pie

This was my grandmotherÂ´s apple pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredientes

  • 1 recipiente pastry for a 9 inche double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brow sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.

Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that ir does not run off.

Bake 15 minutes in the preheated ove. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft
