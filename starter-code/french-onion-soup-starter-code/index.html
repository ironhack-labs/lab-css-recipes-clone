French Onion Soup

We have been trying French onion soup in restaurant for years and my family and friends agree that nothing can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation.

Ingredients

  • - 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • - 2 tablespoons oliveoil
  • - 4 cups sliced onions
  • - 4(10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  • - 2 tablespoons dry sherry(optional)
  • - 1 teaspoon dried thyne
  • - salt and pepper to taste
  • - 4 slices of french bread
  • - 4 slices of provolone cheese
  • - 2 slices of swiss cheese, diced
  • - 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

1) Melt butter with olive oil in an 8 quart stock pot on medium heat. Add onions continually stir under tender and translucent.Do not Brown the onions.

2) Add beef broth, sherry and thyme.Season with salt and pepper,and simmer for 30 minutes.

3) Heat the oven boiler.

4) Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each(bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer).Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone,1/2 slice diced swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place both bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly.
