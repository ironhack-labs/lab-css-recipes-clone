Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 8 granny smith apples -peeled,cored and slice

Directions

  1. 1.Preheat oven to 425 dgerees F(220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flout to form a paste. Add water,white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. 2.Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples,mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust.Pour slowly so that it does not off.
  3. 3.Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
